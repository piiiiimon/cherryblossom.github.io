Lovely, Little, You

I love you not because I think you're good for me, nor because you're the perfect blend of rights and wrongs. I love you not because I expect you to return the favor, I don't.

I love you because you're the one I didn't see but hoped for. You make me feel free to loose out my feelings. The reason why I sometimes ignore the troubles of being uncomfortable.

You always have me at my hearts efforts and wits. You make me wonder what love really is.

The one who made me believe everything I have ever questioned about love. You make me wonder what you mean to me. Running circles around my mind every night, the reason why I lie in bed, but don't want to sleep at night.

I can't seem to shake you off my head. I guess I'm impossibly and inescapably attracted to you. You the simple portrait more beautiful than any art. And I love you for that, I guess I still do.

"I want to be good for you, and say and do all the right things, and be everything you have been looking for because I want you to love me."
