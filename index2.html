TO THE MOON

To The Moon

I wanted to take you with me, To see the beauty beyond ground. But I guess you weren't ready. Yes, you didn't feel the same with me around.

You disagreed without even trying. It's unfair, that's the point of rejection. Although, I couldn't do anything. I wasn't worth your attention.

On my own, I boarded the ship. When I landed on the moon, I still felt incomplete. Why did I have to leave so soon?

I thought I had to be the better one. Though you were far away, I still tried I still shouted to you from the moon, Thinking you'd hear me on the other side.

Guess I was the crazy one.

So, I sat down, as uncertain as I could be, Then I saw the brightest star I could ever see. Among the stars, this Star remains true; That is the love I have for you.

Now I know where to begin. To start to accept and improve. And if I return, maybe I'll ask you again To come with me to the Moon.
